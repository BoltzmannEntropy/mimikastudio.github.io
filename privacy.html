Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us

1. Introduction

MimikaStudio ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our application.

2. Information Collection

MimikaStudio is designed to be privacy-first and local-first. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All voice cloning, text-to-speech synthesis, and audio processing are performed entirely on your device.

3. Voice Sample Processing

Voice samples you provide for cloning are processed locally on your device using on-device AI models. Your voice samples are never uploaded to any cloud service or third-party server. They remain stored only on your device.

4. Text-to-Speech Processing

All text-to-speech synthesis, including with cloned voices, is performed locally using GPU acceleration (MPS on macOS, CUDA on Windows). Your text input is not sent to any external servers.

5. PDF and Audiobook Processing

PDF reading and audiobook creation features process your documents locally. We do not have access to your files or generated audio content.

6. Data Security

Since all data is stored and processed locally on your device, the security of your data relies on the security of your device. We recommend using standard device security features.

7. Third-Party Services

The application may utilize system-level services provided by your operating system. These services are governed by the privacy policies of the respective operating system providers (Apple, Microsoft).

8. Children's Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at tripflow.contact@gmail.com.
